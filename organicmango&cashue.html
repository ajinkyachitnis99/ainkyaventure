Welcome To Yogita Chitnis Mango Farm

Yogita Mango Farm is established In Shiravlli Which is in Ratnagiri District & Khed Taluka in the year 2015.

Since 2015, Yogita Mango Farm has been developing mango trees using sustainable and organic farming methods.

Our mango orchards, shaded by the canopies of these majestic trees, create a serene and inviting atmosphere. The air is perfumed with the sweet fragrance of blooming mango flowers, providing a sensory experience that reflects the purity of our organic practices.

At Yogita Mango Farm, we embrace sustainable agricultural methods, incorporating practices such as crop rotation, composting, and natural pest control. These methods not only contribute to the exceptional quality of our mangoes but also ensure the longevity and resilience of our land.

Why We Focus On Only Organic Mango And Cashew ?

1 Organic mangoes are grown without synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or chemical fertilizers. This means they are less likely to carry pesticide residues, offering a healthier option for consumers. Organic farming methods prioritize soil health, resulting in nutrient-rich produce.

2 Organic farming practices are designed to minimize environmental impact. They avoid the use of synthetic chemicals that can contribute to soil and water pollution. Organic farms often implement techniques such as crop rotation, cover cropping, and natural pest control, promoting a more sustainable and balanced ecosystem.

3 Organic farming typically supports greater biodiversity. By avoiding synthetic chemicals, organic farms provide a healthier habitat for various plants, animals, and beneficial insects. This contributes to the overall resilience of the ecosystem.

4 Many organic enthusiasts argue that organic fruits, including mangoes, often have a richer flavor and superior nutritional content. The focus on soil health and natural inputs can enhance the overall quality of the fruit.
